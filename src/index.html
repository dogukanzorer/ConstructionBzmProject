Our Services

Structural Construction


• Foundation and Groundwork: Excavation of building foundations, pouring foundation concrete, ground improvement, and drainage work.


• Load-Bearing Systems: Construction of reinforced concrete, steel, or wooden load-bearing systems. Construction of columns, beams, slabs, and walls.


• Wall and Roof Work: Building walls with materials such as brick, concrete block, or aerated concrete. Construction of roof beams, roof coverings, and waterproofing.


• Scaffolding and Formwork: Installation and use of formwork systems required for concrete pouring. Installation and use of scaffolding systems throughout construction.

Finish Construction


• Wall and Plaster Work: Plastering of interior and exterior walls, gypsum plaster, and drywall systems.


• Floor Covering Work: Application of flooring materials such as ceramic, parquet, marble, and carpet.


• Ceiling and Suspended Ceiling Work: Gypsum board ceilings, metal ceiling systems, and decorative ceiling applications.


• Painting and Decoration Work: Painting of walls and ceilings, wallpaper application, decorative coatings.


• Carpentry and Furniture Work: Door and window frames, built-in wardrobes, kitchen cabinets, wooden decorations.

Technical Services


• Electrical Installation: Electrical wiring, installation of sockets and switches, installation of lighting systems.


• Plumbing: Installation of water and sewage pipe systems, installation of water fixtures and valves.


• Heating, Cooling, and Ventilation Systems: Installation of heating systems, air conditioning, ventilation ducts, and equipment.


• Fire Suppression and Security Systems: Installation of fire alarm systems, fire suppression systems, security cameras, and other security systems.
